How to Play the Game

  • You entered an unknown realm. As wondrous and surreal as it seems, it is still not your reality. You must try to find a way back to the realm you're most familiar with - reality.
  • Follow the instructions set by the characters you will meet in the game.
  • Do not break lore, play it as it should be played.
  • Do not spoil information for other players.
  • Be a good guest and player, please follow the rules.
  • Most importantly, have fun!
