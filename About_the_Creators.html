About the Creators

Hello! We are Emman Gabriel Ella, Raphael Tabingo, and Johana Todoc. We are the members of CS3 Group 1 and the creators of this game "Escape to Reality". Our goal is to provide a game that will relieve one's boredom and stress and at the same time, allowing them to use their problem solving and critical thinking skills.

We hope that you enjoyed/will enjoy the game we have prepared for you! "Escape to Reality" is a game inspired by : Little Witch in the Woods, Undertale, Stardew Valley, and Fire Emblem. It is a short game comprised of different quests that you must accomplish to help your character escape this dimension and bring them back to the reality they once knew.
